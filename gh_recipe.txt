Indomie Noodles

** Ingredients
- Two sachet of indomie noddle
- Three eggs
- Vegetable eg. onions, tomatoes, cabbage, carrot etc.
- Canned Sardine

** Process
- Cook the indomie for about 7 mins
- Add the sardine to the eggs with tomatoes and onions
- Sramble the eggs
- Finally, add the noodles with the eggs and mixed vegetables and you are good to go.
